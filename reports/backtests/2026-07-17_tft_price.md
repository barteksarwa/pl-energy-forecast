## Verdict

- Long context is REAL but small: rMAE improves monotonically with
  encoder length (0.929 -> 0.879 -> 0.863). The mechanism works.
- It is nowhere near enough: best TFT trails the tabular models by
  ~5.6 EUR/MWh (30%) on the identical window, and does not fix spikes.
- Screening tier: 1 seed, d64 only. A tuned/ensembled TFT would close
  some gap; the EPF literature says not all of it.
- Conclusion unchanged from the worst-day post-mortems: the binding
  constraint is MISSING INFORMATION (outages, fuel), not architecture.
  The outage feature test runs next.
